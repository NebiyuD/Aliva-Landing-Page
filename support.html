Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help you get started

Getting started is easy! Simply request a demo or start your 14-day free trial. Our onboarding team will guide you through the setup process, including EHR integration and staff training.

Aliva integrates with all major EHR systems through FHIR standards, including Epic, Cerner, Meditech, Allscripts, and athenahealth. Custom integrations are available for enterprise customers.

Standard implementations typically take 2-4 weeks depending on your organization's size and complexity. This includes EHR integration, staff training, and workflow customization. Enterprise deployments may require additional time.

You get full access to all features for 14 days with no credit card required. After the trial ends, you can choose a plan that fits your needs or your account will be paused until you subscribe.

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect immediately, and billing is prorated automatically.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), ACH bank transfers, and wire transfers for enterprise accounts. Annual plans can also be paid by invoice.

Aliva uses advanced AI to provide real-time patient data analysis, drug interaction detection, clinical decision support, predictive alerts, and natural language querying of patient records. Our AI is trained on medical literature and continuously updated.

Yes! Aliva automatically scans patient medications and alerts clinicians to potential drug-drug interactions, drug-allergy conflicts, and dosage concerns in real-time. Critical alerts are highlighted for immediate attention.

Aliva is fully responsive and works on any device through your web browser. Native iOS and Android apps are available for Professional and Enterprise plans, offering offline access and push notifications.

Absolutely. Aliva is fully HIPAA compliant with signed Business Associate Agreements (BAA) for all customers. We maintain SOC 2 Type II certification and undergo regular third-party security audits.

All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We use role-based access controls, audit logging, and multi-factor authentication. Enterprise customers can opt for dedicated infrastructure or on-premise deployment.

Data is stored in HIPAA-compliant AWS data centers located in the United States. Enterprise customers can choose specific data residency regions or opt for on-premise deployment for complete control.
